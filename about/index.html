About Me

Student at Carnegie Mellon University, Hangzhou -> California -> Pittsburgh, these has been my go to introduction for years. One day, at a friend’s birthday, as we sat around the couch introducing ourselves with the same format of ‘school, major, year, birthplace’, someone said, ‘Hi I am Jasmine, and I am a happy person’.

Unexpected and new, would say Mary has inspired part of this intro hahah. I wish to use this website to showcase my thoughts, but more importantly, give you a glimpse of who I am.

I like the color green, and tend to listen more than I speak. I enjoy cooking and finding unexpected solutions to things. Ethan Chlebowski is my go to youtuber on food related topics, croque monsieur he taught I have made with every possible bread and cheese.

I also like to draw here and there, though the effort tends to be a, hit or miss lol. I listen to lofi hip hops, currently Iron II in League of Legend, organize my thoughts through Obsidian and Notion, and am a big fan of newsletters.

That’s a bit about me! Kindness, humility, empathy, there is a lot I wish to learn, and welcome to my ongoing journey :).
